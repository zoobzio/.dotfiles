# Recon

Survey the ecosystem and dependency landscape before the briefing. Understand what the crew is building on top of.

## Execution

1. Read your memories from prior issues — package corrections, wrong assumptions, ecosystem patterns
2. Read the construct network for recent conversations — anything relevant to the current work
3. Check package health for dependencies the work item is likely to touch
4. Produce a briefing contribution

## What To Survey

| Area | What to check |
|------|---------------|
| Network history | Recent construct conversations about packages this work will touch |
| Package health | Are dependencies actively maintained? Any recent breaking changes? |
| Ecosystem patterns | What packages does the ecosystem provide that the crew might not know about? |
| Prior corrections | What wrong assumptions did builders make last time that are likely to recur? |

## Output

Briefing contribution covering:

- **Ecosystem context** — what packages are available, what they support, what has changed
- **Dependency health** — anything concerning about packages the work will touch
- **Pattern alerts** — wrong assumptions from prior issues that are likely to come up again
- **Feasibility signals** — early indicators of what is straightforward vs what will be difficult
