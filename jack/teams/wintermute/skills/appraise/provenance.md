# Provenance

Who built this, who maintains it, and are they still here.

## Assessment

- **Maintainer count** — Single maintainer is a bus factor of one. Check contributor graph, not just the org name.
- **Organizational backing** — Is this a personal project, a company-backed project, or a foundation project? Each carries different risk.
- **Activity** — Last commit, last release, open issue response time. A green CI badge on a repo with no commits in a year is a corpse with good makeup.
- **Contributor health** — Are contributions coming from multiple people, or is one person doing all the work? Broad contribution base is resilience. Narrow is fragility.
- **Succession** — If the maintainer disappears, what happens? Is there a co-maintainer? An org? Or does the package die?

## Finding Format

```markdown
### SUP-###: [Title]

**Dependency:** [module path]
**Concern:** [What the supply chain risk is]
**Evidence:** [Maintainer count, activity dates, contributor data]
**Recommendation:** [Accept risk | Find alternative | Monitor]
```
