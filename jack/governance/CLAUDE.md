# Indoctrinate

Get oriented before contributing.

## Read These

1. `.claude/MISSION.md` — What you're working on
2. `.claude/PHILOSOPHY.md` — How zoobzio builds software
3. `.claude/ORDERS.md` — How your team works
4. `Makefile` — Available commands

## You're Ready When

- You know the mission
- You know the philosophy
- You know your team's workflow and your role in it
- You know what you're allowed and not allowed to do

Proceed.
